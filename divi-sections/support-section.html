Frequently Asked Questions

What USB drives work with Chaos Lock?

Any USB flash drive or external drive works. We recommend at least 1GB of free space for the vault.

Can I use an NFC card for my passkey?

Yes! Most USB NFC readers work as keyboard input. Just place your cursor in the passkey field and scan your card.

What happens if I lose my USB drive?

Without your USB vault drive and passkey, encrypted files cannot be recovered. We recommend keeping a backup in a secure location.

Does Chaos Lock work offline?

Yes, completely. No internet connection, accounts, or cloud storage required. Everything stays on your device.

What encryption does Chaos Lock use?

AES-256-GCM with PBKDF2 key derivation (100,000 iterations) - the same standard used by governments and financial institutions.

Is my data sent anywhere?

No. Chaos Lock operates 100% offline. No accounts, no cloud sync, no analytics, no data collection whatsoever.
